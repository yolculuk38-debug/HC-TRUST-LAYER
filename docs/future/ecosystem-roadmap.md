# Ecosystem Roadmap

> **Status:** Idea
>
> **Exploratory / Non-Canonical Notice:** This document is exploratory and non-canonical. It does not define production behavior, modify active HC:// protocol requirements, change active runtime behavior, or change active governance behavior.

## Research Question

What long-term ecosystem directions could HC:// explore after active protocol, runtime, security, and governance foundations are stable?

## Concept

This roadmap keeps broad ecosystem ideas separate from active TODOs. It is a parking area for research themes that need evidence, constraints, and reviewer alignment before any promotion.

Possible long-term themes:

- clearer public verification education;
- mobile-readable review experiences;
- local-first verification package inspection;
- optional ecosystem registries;
- operator-oriented audit and recovery summaries;
- future HC:// Control Panel research; and
- independent reviewer coordination models.

## Boundary Notes

This roadmap does not modify active MVP priorities, runtime behavior, governance behavior, policy enforcement, validators, schemas, signing logic, federation logic, or workflows. It does not claim that listed themes are implemented or committed.

## Open Questions

- Which ecosystem ideas support simple verification flow without adding uncontrolled automation?
- Which ideas should remain documentation-only until stronger evidence exists?
- How can operator tooling stay bounded, explainable, reversible, and human-reviewable?
- What signals would justify promotion from Idea or Research to Candidate?

## Promotion Considerations

Promotion requires a focused proposal, owner or reviewer routing, impact analysis, relevant checks, and human-supervised validation. Promoted items must move into active documentation through a separate, scoped PR rather than by editing this roadmap alone.
