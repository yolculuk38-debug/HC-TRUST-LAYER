# HC-TRUST-LAYER — Content & Abuse Policy

## Purpose

HC-TRUST-LAYER is designed as an open verification and integrity protocol.

The system focuses on transparency, traceability, and record integrity.

---

## Core Principle

HC:// primarily verifies content integrity, not ideological correctness.

Responsibility for published content remains with the original publishers, platforms, or users.

---

## Restricted Usage

HC-TRUST-LAYER may not be used for:

- Child exploitation material
- Human trafficking content
- Terrorism propaganda
- Coordinated violent extremism
- Malware distribution
- Fraud systems
- Identity theft operations
- Illegal surveillance abuse
- Direct incitement of violence

---

## Verification vs Endorsement

Verification records do not automatically mean:

- moral approval
- factual approval
- legal certification
- political endorsement
- religious endorsement

HC:// records only indicate that content integrity records exist within the protocol system.

---

## Regional & Platform Policies

HC-TRUST-LAYER acknowledges that content regulations differ across countries, cultures, and platforms.

Additional policy layers may be implemented by platforms, communities, institutions, or regional deployments.

---

## AI Witness Layer

Advanced AI witness analysis may include:

- manipulation analysis
- deepfake risk detection
- synthetic media indicators
- automated risk scoring

These systems are experimental and may produce errors or uncertainty.
