# HC Multi-AI Council

HC Multi-AI Council is an advisory decision-record layer for HC-TRUST-LAYER.

It is designed to capture structured input from the founder, maintainers, and AI review roles before important project decisions are finalized.

## Purpose

The Council exists to support transparent project decisions.

It does not replace maintainers.

It does not create autonomous authority.

It does not provide a truth guarantee.

## Workflow index

For the full manual Council workflow, read:

- `council/workflow-index.md`

This index connects roles, prompts, checklists, response summaries, intake logs, decision-record update planning, and final human review.

## Current status

The Council is documentation-first and manual.

There is no live AI automation in this layer yet.

There is no LLM voting system.

There is no automatic merge, label, reviewer, or release authority.

## Intended role

A Council record may capture:

- the decision topic
- founder input
- maintainer input
- AI role input
- risks
- alternatives
- final human decision
- follow-up PRs

## Advisory-only boundary

Council outputs are recommendations and decision records.

Human maintainers retain final authority.

## Relationship to HC Control Bot

HC Control Bot is the background GitHub check and advisory scanner.

HC Multi-AI Council is the decision-record layer.

They are separate.

The bot may help surface evidence.

The Council may help organize reasoning.

Neither layer replaces human review.

## Future direction

Future phases may add:

- role definitions
- decision templates
- manual Council records
- hashed decision packets
- audit references
- AI response collection workflows

Any automation must be introduced through separate governance review.
